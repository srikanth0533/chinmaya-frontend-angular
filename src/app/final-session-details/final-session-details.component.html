General Instructions for all participants of GCC 2021 :
  • Please log in at the Reporting time according to the schedule given above
  • Sit comfortably so that you are completely visible.
  • Please ensure that the room has proper lighting and no shadow falls on you.
  • Position your device and adjust your camera in such a way that your posture is clearly seen. Check your net connection and audio device.
  • Your test will be only for a short while. So no other distractions please!
  • In the case of very young children (Nursery to Std. 1), an adult can be there next to the child as a moral support. In the case of children from Std. 2 onwards, an adult can be in the same room, but not within the frame of the camera.
  • Parents, please refrain from prompting or showing the text of the verses to the participants.
  • During a particular time slot, there will be other participants in the room. Hence while chanting of one participant is on, others are requested to mute their microphones. You may keep the video on, but no movements please. This will distract the chanting participant.
  • While chanting, participants are requested not to close their eyes.
  • There is no specific dress code for the participants. They must only be neatly, decently and comfortably clothed. Wearing school uniform is not mandatory.
