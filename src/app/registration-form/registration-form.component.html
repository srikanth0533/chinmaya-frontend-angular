Geeta Chanting Competition - 2021
After the extremely encouraging response received for our first ever Online Geeta Chanting Competition in 2020, Chinmaya Mission, Thane is pleased to announce opening of registrations for GCC 2021, which will again be an online event.
Please read the Instructions carefully and thereafter proceed to fill in the details required in the Registration form and remit the Donation amount.
Geeta Chanting Competition - 2021
INSTRUCTIONS

1. Please fill in all the details in the registration form carefully.

2. Please review the details entered in the form and check if accurate.

3. In case you wish to add more than one participant’s details, please click on “Add more entries” and fill in the details in the second form. Else go to Confirm and Pay.

4. You will be directed to the payment page where you can make the donation of Rs 100 per participant. You can make payment for multiple registrations through a single transaction.

5. Complete the payment using your preferred mode of remittance.

6. You will receive a confirmation email with a PDF file of the receipt once your payment is done.

7. Please note that the Donation made is non-refundable even in the event of non-participation.
